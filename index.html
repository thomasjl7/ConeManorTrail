Cone Manor to Cone Cemetery Trail

About this site

Cone Manor and Cone Cemtery are both historic sites located just off the Blue Ridge Parkway in Blowing Rock. This trail is a good trail for beginners, it's relatively short, and offers great views not far from town.

Start by parking, or walking, next to Cone Manor, and once you've got your fill of the manor, follow the trail past the carriage barn. Shortly after, you'll go under a bridge on the parkway. From here, continue following the trail north as you scale the base of flat top mountain. Finally, you will end up at Cone Cemetery, with a very serene field that overlooks Blowing Rock and the 321 Highway.
